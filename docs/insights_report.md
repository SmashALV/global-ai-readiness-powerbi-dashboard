# Insights Report

## Key Insight 1: Global AI readiness remains concentrated

The United States leads the comparable ranking due to strong performance across Economy, Infrastructure, Innovation, Governance and Talent.

## Key Insight 2: Latin America shows moderate AI readiness

Latin American countries show lower scores than global leaders, especially in Economy and Innovation.

## Key Insight 3: Peru has complete coverage but remains behind regional leaders

Peru has complete pillar coverage, which allows a fair comparison against countries such as Brazil, Chile, Argentina, Colombia and Mexico.

## Key Insight 4: Peru's opportunity areas

Peru's main improvement areas are related to innovation, AI investment and talent development.

## Key Insight 5: Data coverage affects ranking interpretation

Countries with low pillar coverage can appear artificially strong if coverage is not controlled. Therefore, a comparable ranking was created.
